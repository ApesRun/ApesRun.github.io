AIR CRAWL:

AIR CRAWLING IS A GLITCH THAT ALLOWS YOU TO GET HEIGHT INFINITELY:
TO AIR CRAWL YOU FIRST MUST JUMP > DOUBLE JUMP > PULL THE SLINGSHOT BACK > HOLD L3 TO CRAWL > CANCEL OUT OF FIRST PERSON
NOTE THAT LETTING GO OF THE CONTROL STICK OR JUMPING WILL END YOUR AIR CRAWL

IF YOU USE THE SKY FLYER FROM AN AIR CRAWL YOU WILL SNAP DOWN TO THE HEIGHT OF THE INITIAL JUMP, THIS CAN BE USED TO CLIP TROUGH FLOORS
