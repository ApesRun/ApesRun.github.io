NET FLOAT:

FOR A NET FLOAT YOU:
DO A DOUBLE JUMP AND RIGHT AFTER PULL YOUR SLINGSHOT BACK AND GO TO THE GADGET MENU
IN THE GADGET MENU HOLD L3, CLOSE THE MENU AND QUICKLY OPEN IT AGAIN THEN PUT YOUR NET OVER THE SLINGSHOT
HOLD UP ON THE RIGHT STICK AND CLOSE THE MENU.

DOUBLE NET FLOAT:

DO A NET FLOAT AND ABOUT WHEN THE CHARACTER GOES OFF SCREEN
GO TO THE GADGET MENU AND SWITCH YOUR NET FOR THE SLINGSHOT AND HOLD L3
THEN PULL BACK ON THE RIGHT STICK AND CLOSE THE MENU AND QUICKLY OPEN IT AGAIN
AFTER THAT SWITCH THE SLINGSHOT FOR THE NET AND HOLD UP ON RIGHT STICK
