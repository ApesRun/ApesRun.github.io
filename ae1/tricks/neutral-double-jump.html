NEUTRAL DOUBLE JUMP:

NEUTRAL DOUBLE JUMP ALLOWS YOU TO KEEP YOUR MOMENTUM IN A DOUBLE JUMP BY:
JUMPING AND IN MID AIR LETTING GO OF THE CONTROL STICK BEFORE DOING THE DOUBLE JUMP AND ONLY THEN HOLDING THE DIRECTION YOU WANT TO GO.
THIS IS COMMONLY COMBINED WITH THE BOOST JUMP.
