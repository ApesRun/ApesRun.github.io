BOOST JUMP:

BOOST JUMPING IS A TRICK PERFORMED WITH THE DASH HOOP BY:
PRESSING THE BUTTON THE HOOP IS ON FOLLOWED QUICKLY AFTER WITH A JUMP.
THIS TRICK WILL ALLOW YOU TO KEEP THE MOMENTUM OF THE HOOP TROUGH YOUR JUMP.
THIS CAN BE FOLLOWED BY THE INFINITE JUMPS FOR MAXIMUM DISTANCE.
